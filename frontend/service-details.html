Policy Hub

Centralize and enforce cloud governance with pre-built policies for cost and compliance across multi-cloud environments. CloudPi gives FinOps and platform teams a single control plane to standardize guardrails across AWS, Azure, and GCP, so every account stays aligned with budget, security, and operational best practices from day one.

Why governance matters in multi-cloud

As cloud spend scales across AWS, Azure, and GCP, manual oversight breaks down and waste creeps in. The Policy Hub turns governance into a continuous, automated practice: pre-built and custom policies detect cost, security, and configuration drift, then trigger the right remediation before small issues become expensive ones. The result is predictable spend, stronger compliance, and engineering teams that move fast without losing control.

Define and enforce policies

Create and manage policies across cloud providers to ensure compliance with security, cost, and operational best practices.

Remediation workflows

Automate remediation actions for policy violations: stopping instances, modifying configurations, or sending notifications.

Policy as code

Manage and version policies using code for easier deployment, updates, and collaboration.

Continuous compliance

Monitor AWS, Azure, and GCP in real time to catch cost and configuration drift before it impacts your bill.

Which cloud providers does the Policy Hub support?

The Policy Hub works across AWS, Azure, and GCP from a single console, so you can apply consistent cost and compliance guardrails to every account and subscription without juggling separate native tools.

How does automated remediation work?

When a policy violation is detected, CloudPi can trigger remediation workflows automatically, such as stopping idle instances, modifying configurations, or notifying the right team, so waste and risk are addressed without manual intervention.

Can I manage policies as code?

Yes. Policies can be defined, versioned, and deployed as code, making it easy to review changes, collaborate across teams, and roll out governance consistently as your multi-cloud footprint grows.
