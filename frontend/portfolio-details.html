Cutting multi-cloud spend 30% with automated FinOps governance

A global enterprise running production workloads across AWS, Azure and GCP was struggling with fragmented billing, untagged resources, and no clear accountability for cloud spend. Finance and engineering teams worked from conflicting numbers, monthly invoices arrived without context, and runaway costs were only discovered after the fact. Leadership needed a single, trustworthy view of where every dollar was going — and a way to stop overruns before they happened.

CloudPi delivered penny-level cost visibility across all three providers, automated guardrails that enforce budgets and policy in real time, and tag governance that restored accurate allocation even for previously untagged resources. Within two quarters the organization cut cloud spend by roughly 30%, improved cost-allocation accuracy to the team and product level, and brought its multi-cloud estate into continuous compliance.
